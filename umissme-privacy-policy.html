Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the uMissMe app (hereinafter referred to as "Application") for mobile devices. By using this Application, you agree to the collection and use of information as described below.

Information Collection

  • Data Collected: The Application collects only phone numbers from your contacts and they never leave the user’s device unhashed. The hashing process (SHA256) is performed locally on the user’s device in a secure and uncontrolled manner.

Purpose of Data Collection

The hashed phone numbers are uploaded to our servers solely to find potential matches. This process ensures that no identifiable information about your contacts is ever accessible to us.

Data Security

  • Hashing: Phone numbers are hashed using SHA256 before leaving your device. The hashing process is non-reversible, meaning we cannot determine the original phone numbers from the hashed values.
  • Encryption: All data transmissions are encrypted using industry-standard protocols to ensure secure communication between the Application and the server.

Data Deletion

You can delete all uploaded data by logging out of the Application. When you log out, all associated data is removed from our servers.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your contacts or their hashed information with any third party under any circumstances.

Your Consent

By using the Application, you consent to the collection and use of your data as outlined in this Privacy Policy.
